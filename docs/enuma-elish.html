  1. When the heaven above was not named,

    𒍪𒀊𒌝𒈠apsu-um-ma 𒊕𒌅𒌋rish-tu-u 𒍝𒊒𒋗𒌦za-ru-shu-un

    𒄿𒍪uz𒈠i-zu-uz-ma 𒅋𒋫𒋛il-ta-si 𒂊𒇷e-li 𒄯𒈪𒊭har-mi-sha

    𒈪𒈾𒀀mi-na-a 𒉌𒄿𒉡ni-i-nu 𒊭sha 𒉌ip𒁍šamni-ip-pu-sham 𒉡𒍑hallaknu-ush-hal-lak
